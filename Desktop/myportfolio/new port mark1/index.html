MVP automation builds typically take 2-4 weeks. More complex AI agent systems can take 4-8 weeks. I'll give you a realistic timeline upfront based on your requirements.
Do you provide ongoing support?
Yes! I offer maintenance packages to ensure your automations keep running smoothly. This includes monitoring, bug fixes, and improvements as your business needs evolve.

Discovery → Prototype → Build → Test → Deploy → Maintain. I keep you involved at every step and provide regular updates. Most clients see a working prototype within the first week.

Absolutely. I integrate with 200+ platforms including Slack, Notion, Google Workspace, HubSpot, Shopify, and more. If your tool has an API, I can connect it.

NVIDIA NeMo Claw is an enterprise-grade AI agent framework that provides governed, auditable, and production-ready AI agent workflows. It's designed for businesses that need compliance, security, and scalability in their AI systems.

Yes, I specialize in building autonomous agents using OpenAI, Claude, and Gemini, integrated directly into your workflows.
